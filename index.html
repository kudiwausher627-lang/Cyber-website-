What is Hacking

Hacking is the act of gaining unauthorized access to computer systems, networks, or accounts.

How one can be hacked

- Clicking on fake links

- Using weak passwords

- Downloading unsafe files or apps

- Using public Wi-Fi without protection

Ways to avoid being hacked

- Use strong passwords

- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)

- Avoid suspicious links

- Keep software updated

What is Scamming

Scamming is when someone tricks others to steal money, personal information, or data online.

How one can be scammed

- Fake online offers or giveaways

- Phishing emails or messages

- Fake websites or links

- Social media impersonation

Ways to avoid being scammed

- Do not trust unknown links

- Verify websites before entering details

- Never share passwords or OTPs

- Be cautious with online deals
